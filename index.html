How we solve it
How we keep it cold

Cold that survives the grid, in three steps

Everyone else stores power and hopes it lasts. We store the cold itself.

01

Charge

Solar and grid power freeze a phase-change thermal bank inside every unit during the hours power is available.

02

Store

Cold is held as latent heat for many hours with the compressor off, so an outage never means a warm container.

03

Deliver

Product holds its exact temperature band from quay to collection, backed by a monitored uptime guarantee.
